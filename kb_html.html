Kobe Bean Bryant

The man whose competitive spirit was second to none

Timeline of Kobe Bean Bryant

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, son of former NBA player Joe Bryant and mother Pamela Cox Bryant.
  • 1981 - Started playing basketball at the age of three.
  • 1984 - Moved to Reiti, Italy at the age of six after his father retired from NBA.
  • 1991 - Moved back to Philadelphia alongwith his family and was enrolled in eighth grade in Bala Cynwyd Middle School.
  • 1992 - Was enrolled in Lower Merion High School, Ardmore where he earned national recognition for the next 4 years in basketball.
  • 1996 - Drafted in the NBA for the Los Angeles Lakers at the age of 16 for $3.5 million 3-year rookie deal.
  • 1997 - Became the youngest NBA All-Star Slam Dunk contest champion.
  • 2000-2002 - Won his first NBA Championship and later, went on to win consecutively for the next two years acquiring a "3-peat".
  • 2006 - Scored a career-high 81 points in a 122-104 victory over the Toronto Raptors breaking the previous franchise record held by Elgin Baylor for 71 points.
  • 2008 - Was awarded the NBA Most Valuable Player of the season.
  • 2009-2010 - Won his fourth and fifth NBA Championships for the Los Angeles Lakers. He was also awarded the NBA Finals Most Valuable Player for both the years.
  • 2013 - Suffered a devastating achilles injury while playing the 80th season game against the Golden State Warriors.
  • 2016 - Played his last game in the NBA against the Utah Jazz scoring 60 points.
  • 2020 - He died along with his daughter Gianna and seven others in a helicopter crash Calabasas, California.
